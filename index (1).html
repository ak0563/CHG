Our Story

A Heritage of Elegance

"We do not merely host events. We craft memories that endure through generations — woven with grace, adorned with elegance, and delivered with an unwavering commitment to perfection."

The Chaudhary Heritage Group was born from a singular vision — to redefine the landscape of luxury events in India. Rooted in heritage and driven by a passion for refined hospitality, CHG stands as a testament to what is possible when tradition meets contemporary luxury.

From intimate ceremonies to grand celebrations, each CHG venue is a curated world unto itself — where bespoke architecture, immaculate landscaping, and world-class service converge to create moments beyond imagination.
